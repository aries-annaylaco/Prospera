About the project

HubPay's sales team, operating in the high-volume, regulated money-transfer space, needed a focused way to capture, qualify, and track prospects without paying for an enterprise CRM. Off-the-shelf tools like HubSpot and Salesforce are overbuilt for the workflow, while spreadsheets lose context the moment more than one virtual assistant touches the data.

Prospera was built as a lightweight, purpose-fit alternative: daily automated lead-farming on a Supabase backend, two manual entry paths for full operator control, and a unified pipeline dashboard for at-a-glance health.
