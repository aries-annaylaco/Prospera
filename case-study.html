The Problem

HubPay's sales team, operating in the high-volume, regulated money-transfer space, needed a focused way to capture, qualify, and track prospects without paying for an enterprise CRM. Off-the-shelf tools (HubSpot, Salesforce) are overbuilt and expensive for the workflow, while spreadsheets lose context the moment more than one VA touches the data.

The Solution

Prospera is a focused CRM that does three things well:

  • Captures leads automatically on a daily schedule via a Supabase-backed pipeline.
  • Accepts manual entry: CSV bulk import or a quick single-lead form.
  • Visualises the entire sales funnel in a glanceable dashboard.

Key Screens

01Dashboard: at-a-glance pipeline health
Top-level KPIs (active leads, pipeline value, won value, win rate) sit alongside a colour-coded pipeline funnel and a live activity feed. The operator sees the entire book of business in under five seconds.
02Leads: organised, filterable, sourced
Every lead carries company, contact, stage, product, value, source and last-activity date. Search and filter narrow the list instantly; stage tags are colour-coded for triage.
03Dual-path lead entry
Import CSV migrates an existing client list in seconds; + New Lead opens a single-entry form for capturing prospects on the fly. Both feed the same unified leads table.

What a VA Does With This

  • Logs daily incoming leads and tags them by source & stage.
  • Imports client lists handed over from the business owner.
  • Updates pipeline stage as deals progress; flags follow-ups.
  • Reports weekly pipeline value and win-rate to the principal.
